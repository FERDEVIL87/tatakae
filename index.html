Tatakae! Ultraman (1971)
Tatakae! Ultraman (戦え！ウルトラマン, Tatakae! Urutoraman, lit. Fight! Ultraman) was an unused opening theme for the Return of Ultraman TV series, dismissed in favor of the more light-hearted Kaettekita Ultraman. It has however been used in future projects.

Artist: Jiro Dan and Misuzu Children's Choir
Released: 1971
Album: Return of Ultraman Original Soundtrack
